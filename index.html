The Challenge

What's Causing the Delays?

A huge increase in power needs is meeting old, slow systems. This creates a major bottleneck that holds back the entire industry.

The Drivers

Surging Interconnection

A flood of interconnection requests from data centers and renewables.

More Grid Codes

Increasing reliability requirements and compliance standards.

The Bottleneck

The combination of aging tools and inefficient processes create gridlock.

Outdated Software

Clunky and complex interfaces.

Complex Workflows

Slow, manual evaluation processes.

The Outcomes

Project Delays & Backlogs

The interconnection queue grows, stalling vital energy projects and slowing the transition to renewables.

Soaring Costs & Inefficiency

Reduced productivity and project overruns drive up operational expenses for the entire industry.
